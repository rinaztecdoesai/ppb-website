Can you really complete in days?

Yes, because we use cash rather than mortgage finance. If the property is freehold we can complete within 48 hours, dependent on the speed of your solicitors. If the property is a leasehold it can take up to 10 days to purchase the property. The additional time taken is due to obtaining the management pack from the managing agent. We can complete on a timescale that suits you, whether that be days, weeks or months.
